Final Fantasy V Better Job Equipment v2.1 (2022)
-----------------------------------------------
- Knights can equip Axes
- Dragoons can equip Swords (They used to be able to equip swords in previous games)
- White Mages can equip Bows
- Summoners can equip Whips (Like Rydia)
- Mimes can equip any weapon or armor (There is no reason to continue using freelancers)
